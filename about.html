Jason's Humble Beginning

Jason Fulbright

30 July,2019 12:36 pm

I grew up in rural Arkansas on my parent's family farm in the small town of Malvern, Arkansas. I attended my first years of education atBismarck High School, but transferred to a college preparatory school my sophomore year. My new school was the Arkansas School for Mathematics, Sciences, and the Arts, this is where I began taking photography classes and fell in love with art of picture taking. It was also here that I began to learn of the issues that surround us in our everyday lives like climate change, ocean pollution, and widespread inequality. Now Jason aspires to be an underwater photographer to bring awareness to the beauty of the oceans, and why we should protect that beauty for our future generations. Jason is concerned about topics such as the loss of coral reefs, and that is why he is majoring in journalism with a minor in marine policy. Jason currently lives in Miami, Florida where he attends school at the University of Miami.
